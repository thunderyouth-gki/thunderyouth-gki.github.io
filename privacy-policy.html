Privacy Policy

Effective: April 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use thunderyouth-gki.github.io.

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth Login Data: Your email address is collected through Google login for authentication and identification.
  • User-Provided Data: You may optionally provide your full name, gender, and birthdate. This is not mandatory to access the website.
  • Submitted Content: Images and forms you upload or submit will be stored securely.

2. How We Use the Data

  • To securely identify users using Google OAuth.
  • To record participation in youth activities.
  • To store uploaded images and form responses in Google Drive and Google Sheets.

3. Data Storage

  • Google Sheets: Used to store text responses and optional user info.
  • Google Drive: Used to store media uploads (e.g., pictures).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Data is accessible only by authorized site administrators.

5. Your Rights

  • You may contact us to access or update your information.
  • You can request data deletion at any time.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, using trusted platforms like Google OAuth, Google Drive, and Google Sheets.
